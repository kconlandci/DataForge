DataForge - Delete Your Data

Last updated: March 2026

What data is stored?

DataForge stores lab progress, scores, XP, streak history, and related usage data locally on your device. The app also keeps an anonymous Firebase UID for entitlement and session continuity.

How to delete your data

You can delete all DataForge progress directly from the app:

1 Open DataForge
2 Open the Settings tab
3 Tap Reset Progress
4 Confirm Reset Everything

This permanently removes locally stored progress, scores, and streak data from the app.
