DataForge Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data Storage

DataForge stores progress data such as completed labs, scores, XP, and streaks locally on your device.

2. Anonymous Authentication

DataForge uses Firebase Anonymous Authentication to generate a random anonymous UID used for entitlement lookup and app continuity. This UID is not linked to your name, email address, or phone number.

3. Purchase Processing

If you purchase DataForge Premium, RevenueCat processes entitlement verification using your anonymous UID and store receipt. Payment details are handled by the app store provider.

4. Usage Analytics

DataForge records anonymous in-app analytics such as labs started, labs completed, and session duration to support recommendations and progress tracking.

5. Children's Privacy

DataForge is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

6. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy can be directed to dataforge.app@gmail.com.
